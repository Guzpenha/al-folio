Gustavo Penha

I am a research scientist at Spotify with a PhD from TU Delft in conversational search and recommendation. My research interests include information retrieval, recommender systems, natural language processing, and machine learning. I am currently working with information filtering systems that generally perform recommendation and search. In this context, some techniques and topics that I work with are dense/generative recommendation & retrieval models, re-rankers, LLMs, alignment, explainability, negative sampling, synthetic data generation, knowledge distillation, Generation-Augmented-Retrieval, and RAG.
